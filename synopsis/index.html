Synopsis

Two young men from different backgrounds meet on a night out and form a deep connection, but their burgeoning romance is tested by the harsh realities of their lives and the expectations of those around them.

As they navigate their feelings for each other, they must also confront their own identities and the challenges of being gay in a world that doesn't always accept them. Will their love be strong enough to overcome the obstacles in their path?
