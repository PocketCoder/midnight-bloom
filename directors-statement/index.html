Director's Statement

Midnight Bloom is a film about the transformative power of love and the courage it takes to be true to yourself. It's a story that I've wanted to tell for a long time, and I'm thrilled to be bringing it to life with such a talented team.

I want to create a film that is both visually stunning and emotionally resonant, a film that will stay with audiences long after they've left the cinema. I want to tell a story that is both specific and universal, a story that will speak to anyone who has ever felt like an outsider.
